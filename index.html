Event Venue

Event venue location info and gallery

MES College of Engineering, Kuttippuram

Situated on the alluring and serene banks of Bharathapuzha, MES College of Engineering is the first Engineering College found under the self-financing sector of Kerala. Established as an institution with minority status in 1994, MESCE is widely acknowledged as one of the chief institutions imparting technical education in the state. One of the oldest self-financing colleges in Kerala, the college offers admission to all categories of students including special consideration to the educationally backward communities.

F.A.Q

  • What is Frizbee,21?

    After the nerving buzz of the pandemic, FrizBee'21 announces it's flamboyant arrival as the year's legendary flagship event of IEEE SB MESCE, continuing it's saga in '21. Bringing you it's fifth sequel, The All India level event is scheduled to be held from 17th to 19th of December 2021. In addition to the multitudinous workshops and inspiring talks, we make it our priority to provide a platform in enriching students to increase their skills and develop innovation.

  • Who can attend FrizBee’21?

    Engineering students and young professionals across India who are interested in Artificial Intelligence, Augmented Reality, IoT and who wish to become an entrepreneur and learn the strategies of business and business intelligence can attend FrizBee’21. We grant you for a hands-on session in all the above-mentioned fields.

  • How do students or young professionals benefit from FrizBee’21?

    FrizBee’21 is a brilliant platform for the professional members across India to collaborate with the challenging members from academics and industry. There will be interactive and innovative sessions for every individual and hands-on sessions for every track you have opted for.

  • What is the theme of the FrizBee’21?

    The theme is “Think Create. Innovate.

  • Is there any possibility for extension of accommodation?

    There is no possibility of extended accommodation. But accommodation will be provided for all the participants all through the event.

  • Is there any occurrence of parallel sessions?

    Various parallel tracks will be held which will include tracks such as IOT/Augmented Reality/artificial intelligence.

  • Can I get any discount during registration?

    Discount offers may be given to IEEE members.

  • For attending the event are there any mandatory requirements regarding IEEE?

    FrizBee’21 is also open for Non-IEEE members. and students all around the nation. Hence this is open to everyone. If you are an IEEE, then their membership number should be given as the proof.
